# Players, Ratings & Coaching Overhaul

Make players feel distinct, make coaching choices matter more and visibly shape
play, and let the user see every rating and the buffs/debuffs applied to it.

Status: ⬜ planned · 🚧 in progress · ✅ done

## P1 — Player & team identity ✅
- Team **strength** rating: library teams are curated (powerhouses → underdogs);
  custom teams derive a stable strength. Team choice now changes roster quality.
- Rosters generated deterministically from **team identity + seed** (a team plays
  the same every time) with a wider talent spread and one **star** per unit.
- Difficulty folds into the AI team's strength.

## P2 — Deeper coaching + bigger effects ✅
- More game-plan sliders: **Tempo** (offense) and **Press** (defense) added to
  air / explosive / coverage / pressure.
- Buffs/debuffs hit harder (larger rating swings) and touch more ratings.
- New axes affect *play*, not just ratings:
  - **Tempo** → no-huddle burns less clock (more plays) / huddle drains it.
  - **Press** → tighter man coverage early, but more risk deep.
- Rating gaps **matter more**: widened contest curves in the engine so a stud
  beats a scrub decisively.

## P3 — Ratings visibility ✅
- A **Roster / Ratings** overlay: every player's ratings with base → effective
  and the game-plan **+buff / −debuff** shown, plus an OVR per player and team.
  Reachable from setup (scout your team) and in-game (dock button).
